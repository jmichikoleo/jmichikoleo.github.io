About Me

I am an Integrated Master's–Ph.D. student in Industrial Data Science and Engineering at Pukyong National University and Pusan National University (Joint Degree Program), with a background in Big Data Statistics and Business Administration from Kyungsung University.

My work focuses on Operations Research and data-driven optimization, with particular interests in routing, scheduling, and decision-making under constraints. I explore how traditional optimization methods can be combined with machine learning and large language models (LLMs) to address complex industrial and real-world problems.

I have experience in Python and R for statistical modeling, as well as tools such as SAS and SPSS. My background in marketing and economics complements my technical work, allowing me to approach optimization problems with both analytical rigor and business context.

Currently, I am expanding my expertise in financial analysis through the CFA program, while exploring applications of optimization and data science in logistics, finance, and intelligent decision systems.

I am particularly interested in research and collaboration opportunities at the intersection of optimization, AI, and real-world systems.
